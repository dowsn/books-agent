Upload your book cover image here!

Instructions:
1. Click the three dots next to this folder
2. Select "Upload file"
3. Choose your book cover image
4. Rename it to "book.png" (or book.jpg)
5. Click "Run" to extract ISBN and book information

For best results:
- Use the back cover of the book (where the ISBN barcode is)
- Make sure the image is clear and well-lit
- The ISBN should be visible in the image
